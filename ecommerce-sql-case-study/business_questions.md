# 📌 Business Questions

This project aims to solve the following business problems using SQL:

## Customer Analysis
1. Identify top 3 cities with highest customer base
2. Analyze customer order frequency distribution
3. Identify dominant customer purchase segments
4. Analyze customer acquisition trends

## Product Analysis
5. Identify high-value products based on purchase behavior
6. Find product categories with highest customer reach
7. Identify low engagement products (purchased by <40% of customers)

## Sales Analysis
8. Analyze month-on-month sales trends
9. Identify the month with the largest revenue decline
10. Analyze average order value fluctuations
11. Identify peak sales months

## Inventory & Operations
12. Identify products with highest turnover rate
13. Determine months requiring higher inventory levels
