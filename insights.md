# 📊 Key Insights & Business Impact

## Customer Insights
- Delhi, Chennai, and Jaipur have the highest customer concentration  
→ Focus marketing efforts in these regions  

- Occasional shoppers form the largest segment  
→ Opportunity to convert them into repeat customers  

---

## Product Insights
- Electronics category attracts the highest number of customers  
→ Continue investment in this category  

- Some products are purchased by less than 40% of customers  
→ Improve visibility or reconsider inventory  

---

## Sales Insights
- Sales fluctuate across months instead of steady growth  
→ Requires better promotional strategy  

- February 2024 saw the largest decline in sales  
→ Investigate external or operational causes  

- Peak sales months: September and December  
→ Plan inventory and staffing accordingly  

---

## Inventory Insights
- Product ID 7 has the highest turnover rate  
→ Ensure consistent stock availability
